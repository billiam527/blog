Data Visualization

September 25, 2017 Bill

1 minute read

Today we are going to go over some basic data visualization techniques using the Lahman dataset, dplyr, ggplot2, and ggiraph. Our goal will be to create a scatter plot that shows elite homerun hitters (that we will define as >= 400 career homeruns) and a comparison of there strikeouts. First, we must filter and summarize hitters that were able to hit 400 homeruns in their careers. This gives us a list of players who were able to hit 400 or more homeruns.
